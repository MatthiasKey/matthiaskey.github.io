Overview

The Book Of Revelation is Matthias Key's arrangement of this last book of the New Testament into chronological order, based on the Cyber Key that he developed for his landmark book Letter to the Tribulation.

Features of this book

  • Chronological order rendering
    • arranged by Cyber Key sections K00 through K50
    • verses with double and triple fulfillment indicated by a double dagger mark ‡ that points to the other sections where they occur, for example, 12:3‡K04,K15
    • print edition cross-references in middle margin
    • e-book cross-references link to non-Revelation verses courtesy of BibleGateway.com
    • a portion of a verse that belongs to elsewhere in the timeline will be in small italics:
      12:4‡K01,K15And his tail drew the third part of the stars of heaven, and did cast them to the earth: and the dragon stood before the woman which was ready to be delivered, for to devour her child as soon as it was born.
  • Verse order rendering
    • with KJV section headings
    • horizontal rule dividers between verses that spiral in the chronology
    • corresponding Cyber Key sections K00 through K50 given in middle margin
