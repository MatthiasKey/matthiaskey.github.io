What Makes This Book Unique

Prophecies of the Mahdi that predate Muhammad

The Islamic prophet Muhammad is not the first to predict the coming of an end-time Islamic messianic figure, known to Muslims as the Mahdi. In Ancient Prophecies of the Mahdi, Matthias shows how the Bible book of Revelation has veiled prophecies of a figure strikingly like the Mahdi, called the beast.

Chronological Clarity

There is consensus among Islamic teachers regarding main events of the end-times, as prophesied by Muhammad in the Hadiths. But there is not agreement on the sequence of subsidiary events. The Cyber Key of Revelation uncovers a plausible and detailed chronological order of all distinct end-time Hadiths. It should benefit Muslims in understanding their prophecies and their true origin.
